Installation
 
Download the app from the following URL:

http://ferrolab.dmi.unict.it/netmatch/download.html


Open the "App Manager" from "Apps" menu in Cytoscape (Fig. 1) and select "Install From File..." (Fig. 2).

After a few seconds NetMatch* will be installed as app in Cytoscape (Fig. 3).
